How to Use HomeCast

What is HomeCast?

HomeCast turns any phone or laptop into a security camera. Another device on the same Wi-Fi network can watch the live video feed in real time.

What You Need

  • 1 Two devices — one acts as the camera, the other watches
  • 2 Same network — both must be on the same Wi-Fi or hotspot

Step-by-Step

1

Set up the Camera

On your first device (e.g. an old phone), tap "Act as Camera" and allow camera access. A 4-digit PIN will appear.

2

Connect the Viewer

On your second device, tap "Viewer Dashboard", enter the 4-digit PIN, and tap Connect.

3

Watch Live!

The live video will appear. Press F for fullscreen. The stream is encrypted and goes directly between your devices.

Tips

  • • Place the camera device in a steady position, facing what you want to monitor
  • • Keep the camera device plugged in — the screen stays on to prevent sleeping
  • • The rear camera is used by default for better quality
  • • Multiple viewers can connect using the same PIN
  • • No data is stored or recorded — it's live streaming only

Important

Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network (or connected to the same phone hotspot). The video won't work if one device is on Wi-Fi and the other is on mobile data.
